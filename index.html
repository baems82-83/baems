About the program

Built around the NEB syllabus — and what comes after it

Most students in this stream aren't just clearing board exams; they're aiming at CEE, IOE, IOM or CMAT the same year. We plan both from day one instead of treating entrance prep as an afterthought in the second year.

Classes run in small sections so lab time is real lab time, not a demonstration from the back row. Every unit test is set in board-exam format so students walk into Grade 12 finals having already sat one two dozen times over.

  • Compulsory: English, Nepali
  • Major: Physics, Chemistry, Biology or Computer Science, Mathematics
  • Weekly diagnostic tests with NEB-style marking
  • Separate CEE / IOE / IOM entrance batch, evenings
  • Fully equipped Physics, Chemistry and Biology labs
